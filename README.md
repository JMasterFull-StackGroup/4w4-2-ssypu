# <summary> 简介 </summary>

西交利物浦大学科技创新协会 

（Science Innovation Association）

西交利物浦大学科技创新协会成立于2009年，

由热爱科技创新及科技实践事业的西交利物浦大学青年学生组成，

是学校最早成立的组织类社团之一，

也是目前最大的科技创新方向组织类社团。

它立足本校，面向社会，

负责我校学生课外学术科技活动的组织管理工作，

主要专注于科研与科普。

Science and innovation association was established in 2009 by students who are passionate about science, technology and innovation. It is one of the earliest and the largest Functional club for science in XJTLU. It is responsible for the organization and management of extracurricular scientific and technological activities for students, mainly focusing on scientific research and popularization of science.

# <summary> 部门组成 </summary>

> 1. 【科研中心】
	
	科研中心主要负责科协的学术以及科研工作，包括对新人的教学和各种科研项目的推进。
	
	Research Centre: The Research Centre is responsible for the academic and research work of the Association, including the teaching of newcomers and the promotion of various research projects.

> 2. 【外联部】

	主要负责多渠道地筹集活动资金，以保证各类科研活动的顺利开展，另外负责对外联络，拓展渠道，承接项目，推广协会的科研成果。

	The Outreach Department is mainly responsible for raising funds for activities to ensure the smooth running of various scientific activities, as well as liaising with the outside world, developing channels, undertaking projects and promoting the scientific achievements of the Association.

> 3. 【综管部】

	基础部门，在这里的主要工作就是协助创意部的小伙伴们开展日常活动，并做好前期准备，比如写预算报表，工作分配，预定教室等。

	The General Management Department is the basic department of the Association, and its main work here is to assist the Creative Department in carrying out daily activities and making preparations, such as writing budget statements, assigning work and reserving classrooms.

> 4. 【创意宣传部】

	主要负责社团活动的策划，团建活动的组织，推文、海报、视频等宣传材料的制作，以及公众号的运营。

	The Creative Publicity Department is mainly responsible for the planning of society activities, the organisation of group building activities, the production of promotional materials such as tweets, posters and videos, and the operation of public websites.

> 5. 【Apple活动中心】

	作为科技创新协会的下属社团，iOS Club 是由 Apple 认证的大学生 iOS 开发者俱乐部，也是西交利物浦大学的 Apple 爱好者社团。

	The iOS Club is an Apple-certified club for university iOS developers and the Apple Enthusiasts' Club at XJTLU. 

> 6. 【IEEE学生分会活动中心】

	电气和电子工程师协会（Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers）是世界上最大的非营利性专业技术学会。IEEE致力于电气、电子、计算机工程和与科学有关的领域的开发和研究，在航空航天、信息技术、电力及消费性电子产品等领域已制定了900多个行业标准，是具有较大影响力的国际学术组织。

	The institute of electrical and electronics engineers is the world's largest nonprofit professional technology institute.IEEE is dedicated to the development and research of electrical, electronic, computer engineering, and science-related fields, and has developed more than 900 industry standards in the fields of aerospace, information technology, power, and consumer electronics.

> 7. 【Matrix Laboratory 活动中心】

	Matrix Laboratory 作为隶属于科技创新协会的下属社团，得到来自 MathWorks 的授权与支持，致力于为同学们提供优质的学习资源、专业的学术讲座和竞赛，以及良好氛围的学习社群，为每一位同学提供发挥创造力的机会。

	Matrix Laboratory is an affiliate of the ScienceInnovation Association and is supported by MathWorks. It is dedicated to providing students with access to quality learning resources, professional academic lectures and competitions, and a welcoming learning community that offers opportunities for creativity.
